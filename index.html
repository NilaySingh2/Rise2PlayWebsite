Who We Are

Rise2Play is a youth-led nonprofit dedicated to breaking down the financial barriers that prevent children from participating in sports. Through equipment drives, free clinics, and school partnerships, we ensure every kid can discover the discipline, teamwork, and joy that athletics provide.

Founded by passionate young athletes, Rise2Play understands firsthand the life-changing impact sports can have. Talent is everywhere. Opportunity should be too.

Our Mission

Rise2Play is driven by a simple belief: every child deserves the chance to play, grow, and thrive through sports.

Rise2Play exists to ensure every child, regardless of socioeconomic background, has equal access to sports and the life skills they foster. We collect, refurbish, and distribute sports equipment to underserved communities, removing financial barriers that sideline talented, eager young athletes.

A world where no child sits on the sidelines simply because they cannot afford to play. We envision communities where youth sports are universally accessible, where every child experiences the camaraderie, discipline, resilience, and joy that athletics bring.

  • Equipment Drives — Community collections of donated sports gear.
  • Equipment Distribution — Items sorted and delivered directly to children and schools.
  • Sports Clinics — Free clinics where kids learn new sports and receive coaching.
  • Community Partnerships — Collaborations with local schools, rec centers, and clubs.
  • Awareness Campaigns — Raising awareness about youth sports inequality.

Research shows youth sports improve academic performance, mental health, and social skills. They teach teamwork, perseverance, and leadership. Yet millions in underserved communities never get these benefits. Rise2Play is changing that.

  • Equity — Equal opportunity for all children.
  • Community — Strengthening neighborhoods through shared athletic experiences.
  • Empowerment — Equipping kids with tools for success on and off the field.
  • Compassion — Leading with empathy in everything we do.
  • Passion — Driven by love of the game and the children we serve.

Frequently Asked Questions

Rise2Play is a youth-led nonprofit giving underprivileged children access to sports and equipment. We believe every child deserves to play, compete, and grow through athletics regardless of financial background.

Drop off gently used or new sports equipment at our collection drives, or email rise2playnp@gmail.com to arrange a pickup or mail-in donation. We accept gear for all sports including balls, jerseys, shoes, helmets, and more.

Fill out our volunteer form or email rise2playnp@gmail.com. We welcome volunteers for equipment drives, sports clinics, and community events.

Soccer, basketball, baseball, football, tennis, swimming, track and field, and more. Our goal is to match equipment to whatever sport a child wants to pursue.

100% of equipment donations go directly to children in need. Monetary donations cover operational costs, event hosting, and purchasing new equipment when items are not available through drives.

Rise2Play currently serves communities locally with plans to expand regionally. Follow @rise2playfoundation on Instagram for updates on new locations and initiatives.
